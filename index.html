About Me

I'm a full-stack software engineer specializing in C#, .NET, Angular, and Azure. My journey into software engineering is unique: I spent 10 years as a high school counselor and 4 years as a music teacher, helping hundreds of students navigate their futures. That experience taught me to understand users deeply and build solutions that truly serve their needs.

Today, I bring that human-centered perspective to every line of code I write. As the sole engineer at Anchovy, I've grown our mobile app from 0 to 3,600+ users and 20,000+ recipes by architecting scalable systems and prioritizing user experience. Whether building WPF desktop applications or cloud-based APIs, I combine technical expertise with empathy for the people using the software.
